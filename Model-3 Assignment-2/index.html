Aloo Gobi

CHEF NAME: Aditi Srivastava
INGREDIENTS:
  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • 2 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
PROCEDURE:
  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add tomatoes and spices, cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add potatoes and cauliflower, mix well.
  5. Cover and cook until veggies are tender.
  6. Sprinkle garam masala, mix, and serve hot.

Paneer Butter Masala

CHEF NAME: Yana Singh
INGREDIENTS
  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup cashew cream
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter
PREPARATION
  1. Sauté onions until golden brown, add ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  3. Add spices, cashew cream, and paneer cubes.
  4. Cook until the gravy thickens and paneer is soft.
  5. Add butter, garam masala, and salt to taste.
  6. Simmer for a few minutes, serve hot.

Dal Tadka

CHEF NAME: Manorama Ji
INGREDIENTS:
  • 1 cup yellow lentils (dal)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp ghee
PREPARATION:
  1. Cook lentils with turmeric until soft.
  2. Heat ghee, add cumin and mustard seeds.
  3. Sauté onions until golden, add tomatoes and spices.
  4. Add cooked lentils, simmer until flavors blend.
  5. Garnish with coriander, serve hot.
